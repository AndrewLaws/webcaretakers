All calculators

Every calculator on the site, grouped by category. More are added as they are built. If a category looks empty, it is because we have not finished the first tool in it yet, not because we have forgotten.

AI

Tools for working with AI APIs and large language models.

  • LLM Token Usage Calculator

    Estimate the per-call, daily, monthly and annual cost of an LLM workload across Claude, GPT, Gemini and DeepSeek. Side-by-side comparison, baked-in pricing dated for transparency, browser-only.

  • AI Image Generation Cost Calculator

    Estimate the per-image, monthly and annual cost of generating AI images. Side-by-side comparison across DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion XL, Imagen 3 and Flux Pro, with pricing dated for transparency.

  • RAG Pipeline Cost Calculator

    Estimate the monthly cost of running a Retrieval-Augmented Generation pipeline. Embedding, vector database, and LLM query costs broken out, with a month-by-month projection and an LLM swap comparison.

  • AI vs Human Writer Cost Calculator

    Compare the monthly cost of producing written content via an LLM against a human writer. Per-word or per-hour human rates, side-by-side model table, honest about editorial trade-offs.

Broadband

Work out the speed, bandwidth, and plan that actually fits your household or office.

  • Broadband Bandwidth Calculator

    Estimate the download and upload speeds your household needs based on streaming, video calls, gaming, smart devices, and remote work.

  • Web Hosting Storage & Bandwidth Estimator

    Work out how much storage and monthly bandwidth your website needs based on your traffic, page weight, media files, and downloadable content. See which hosting tier fits.

Business

Return on investment, tax obligations, marketing performance and web performance.

  • ROI Calculator

    Work out return on investment as a percentage, net profit, and multiple of money. Add a time period to see the annualised return (CAGR). Works for any investment or spend.

  • SEO ROI Calculator

    Enter your monthly SEO cost, current and projected organic sessions, and conversion data to see ROI, monthly profit, and the minimum traffic needed to break even.

  • Email Marketing ROI Calculator

    Enter your list size, send frequency, open and click rates, and conversion data to see ROI, cost per conversion, and the minimum list size needed to cover your costs.

  • UK VAT Calculator

    Add VAT to a net price or remove it from a gross price. Covers all three UK rates: standard (20%), reduced (5%) and zero (0%). Net, VAT amount and gross shown with full workings.

  • Website Performance Budget Calculator

    Set concrete page-weight and Core Web Vitals targets for your site type and audience. Add your current load time and revenue figures to see what a slow site is costing you in lost conversions.

  • Profit Margin Calculator

    Calculate gross and net profit margin from revenue and costs, or find the selling price needed to hit a target margin. Shows the difference between margin and markup.

  • Freelance Day Rate Calculator

    Work out the day rate you need to charge as a freelancer. Accounts for non-billable days, expenses, pension, and gaps between clients.

  • Break-Even Calculator

    Find the number of sales needed to cover your costs. Set a profit target to see units required to hit it. Includes a scenario table at different volumes.

  • IT Support Build vs Buy Calculator

    Compare the annual cost of hiring in-house IT support against a managed service. Shows the verdict at your headcount, break-even users, and full workings.

  • Domain Renewal True Cost Calculator

    See the real multi-year cost of a domain once promo pricing ends and add-ons (privacy, email, DNS, SSL) are included. Optional comparison against a cheaper registrar.

Conversions

Metric-imperial converters for everyday units.

  • Unit Converter

    Length, mass, volume, temperature, speed and area. Pick a category, enter a value, see every common unit at once. Metric and imperial side by side.

  • Cooking Measurements Converter

    Convert cooking measurements across UK, US and metric conventions. Cups, tablespoons, fluid ounces, grams, ounces, gas mark to Celsius, plus cup-to-grams densities for the ingredients that actually matter.

Finance

Sensible estimates for personal money decisions.

  • US Mortgage Calculator

    Work out your monthly US mortgage payment with principal, interest, property tax, home insurance, HOA and PMI broken out separately. Total interest and total cost over the life of the loan.

  • UK Mortgage Calculator

    Work out a UK monthly mortgage payment during the fixed-rate period and after it reverts to the lender's Standard Variable Rate, plus stamp duty and all the cash you need on completion day.

  • Loan Calculator

    Work out the monthly payment, total interest and total cost of any fixed-rate personal, auto or student loan. See how much you save by paying extra each month. Currency is a toggle.

  • Compound Interest Calculator

    See how a starting balance and regular contributions grow with compound interest. Optional inflation adjustment, year-by-year breakdown, currency toggle.

  • UK Salary Tax Calculator

    Enter your gross salary to see income tax, National Insurance and student loan deductions broken down, plus take-home by year, month and week. England, Wales and Northern Ireland rates.

  • UK Stamp Duty Calculator

    Calculate Stamp Duty Land Tax for a residential purchase in England or Northern Ireland. Covers standard purchases, first-time buyer relief and the additional property surcharge. Full band breakdown.

  • Debt Payoff Calculator

    Work out how long it takes to pay off a credit card, loan, or overdraft at a fixed monthly payment. Add an extra monthly payment to see exactly how much time and interest you save.

  • Tip Calculator

    Work out the tip, split the bill, and optionally round each person up to a whole number so nobody has to fish out coins. Covers USD, GBP, EUR, CAD and AUD.

  • Savings Goal Calculator

    Work out the monthly number you need to hit a savings target by a specific date. Factors in your starting balance and the interest your account pays.

Health

Quick health and fitness numbers, with the caveats made plain.

  • BMI Calculator

    Work out your body mass index in metric or imperial units, with the WHO category and an honest note on what BMI can and cannot tell you.

  • Calorie Calculator

    Calculate your basal metabolic rate and daily calorie target based on your activity level and goal. Includes macro split and a projected timeline for weight change goals.

  • Running Pace Calculator

    Work out your running pace, finish time, or distance covered. Enter any two of pace, distance, and time. Metric and imperial, with race time predictions for 5K, 10K, half marathon and marathon.

  • TDEE Calculator

    Work out Total Daily Energy Expenditure and BMR using Mifflin-St Jeor, Harris-Benedict or Katch-McArdle. Includes cut and bulk targets and a starting macro split.

    Work out your estimated due date from your last period, conception date, or IVF transfer date. Shows current gestational age, trimester, and key milestone dates.
