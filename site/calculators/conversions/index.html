Conversion calculators

Convert between metric and imperial units for the things that come up in everyday life: distances, weights, temperatures, volumes, speeds and areas. One tool, one input, all the sensible outputs at once.

  • Unit Converter

    Length, mass, volume, temperature, speed and area. Pick a category, enter a value, see every common unit at the same time. Metric and imperial in one place.

  • Cooking Measurements Converter

    Cups, tablespoons, fluid ounces and grams across UK, US and metric. Gas mark to Celsius. A cup-to-grams block for plain flour, sugars, butter, oats, rice and the rest.

  • Time Zone Converter

    Convert a date and time between IANA zones. Pick a source, add as many targets as you need, see day-of-week, local time and UTC offset for each. DST handled by your OS.

Why yet another unit converter

Most converters on the internet make you pick the "to" unit before they'll show you anything. That's fine if you already know which one you want. But half the time you're converting because you're not sure, and you want to see "6 foot 2 in stones" and "6 foot 2 in metres" side by side without clicking twice. This one gives you every unit at once.

It also runs in the browser. Nothing uploaded, no account, no ads shoved between the input and the result.
